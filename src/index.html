About Us

Welcome to the About Us page of Capstone Research Group 4 from Grade 12, Section St. Hildegard,
at Holy Family Academy High School. We are a dedicated team of students passionate about making a
positive impact on our school environment. Our journey revolves around the development of an app,
which serves as the culmination of our capstone research project. As stewards of our environment,
we recognize the importance of addressing the issue of food waste.

Our primary goal is to develop an innovative app that not only contributes to a greener planet but also
supports the cleanliness and health of our beloved school. By harnessing technology, we aim to foster a
culture of responsibility and stewardship among our fellow students. Join us on this exciting endeavor as
we strive to make a meaningful difference. Together, let's embrace the power of innovation to create a
cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable school environment.
